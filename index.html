The onboarding

Before we coach, we listen.
Week one is the diagnostic.

Most consultants show up with a framework built for someone else. The Floor shows up with questions — and by Friday, you have a map of your team specifically.

MON

Day 1 — Monday

Owner intake call

60-minute deep dive. We cover the business context, revenue goals, team history, and what's broken. By the end, we know what success looks like in 90 days and what's gotten in the way before.

Owner · 60 min Jeff leads
TUE

Day 2 — Tuesday

Seller onboarding + baseline assessment

Each seller gets a 30-minute welcome call — not a performance review, a conversation. What do they love about selling? Where do they feel stuck? The AI layer also deploys their first self-assessment: communication style, stress response, and pipeline habits.

Sellers · 30 min each AI self-assessment Jeff leads
WED

Day 3 — Wednesday

Shadow day — listen before you coach

Jeff reviews 2–3 recorded calls or email threads per seller. No feedback yet — just observation. The AI flags patterns: filler language, missed discovery, late follow-ups, tone drift. Jeff annotates for the context a machine can't read.

Call + email analysis Jeff annotates
THU

Day 4 — Thursday

AI daily rhythm goes live

Sellers receive their first morning check-in: one priority, one focus question, one accountability prompt. It's not a task list — it's a management voice in their day. The system logs, flags anomalies, and starts building the baseline.

Daily check-in live First seller responses
FRI

Day 5 — Friday

Diagnostic readout with the owner

Jeff delivers a plain-language summary: seller strengths, coaching priorities, messaging gaps, and the 30-day plan. No deck, no jargon. Just clarity. The owner leaves knowing exactly what The Floor is going to fix — and how.
