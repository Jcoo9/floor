AI in practice

Not AI as a buzzword.
AI as a management tool.

Every deliverable The Floor provides is made sharper, faster, and more precise by AI. Here's exactly how it works in your team's day to day.

CALL INTELLIGENCE
Every sales call
AI transcribes and analyzes every seller call — talk/listen ratio, filler word frequency, discovery question depth, objection patterns, sentiment shifts. Jeff reviews the data and builds individual coaching from what actually happened on the call, not what the seller remembers.
BEHAVIORAL PATTERNS
Across weeks and months
AI tracks patterns across daily check-in responses over time — who's consistently avoiding pipeline conversations, who's showing early signs of disengagement, where team morale is shifting before it becomes a performance problem. Human judgment interprets what the data means and what to do about it.
PIPELINE SYNTHESIS
Weekly owner summary
AI aggregates CRM data, seller check-in responses, call analysis, and pipeline movement into a single weekly summary. Jeff adds the human read — what it means, what's at risk, what needs your attention. Plain language. No new dashboards. Delivered every Friday.
COACHING PERSONALIZATION
Every 1:1 session
Before every bi-weekly 1:1, AI summarizes that seller's call data, check-in patterns, and pipeline activity since the last session. Jeff walks into every coaching conversation with a complete picture of exactly where that person is — and what they need most right now.
