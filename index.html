The onboarding

Before we coach, we listen.
Week one is the diagnostic.

Most consultants show up with a framework built for someone else's team. The Floor shows up with questions — and by Friday, you have a clear picture of exactly what's getting in the way of your number.

MON

Day 1 — Monday

Owner intake call

60-minute deep dive. Revenue goals, team history, what's broken, and what success looks like in 90 days. Jeff listens more than he talks. By the end, we know exactly what we're solving for.

Owner · 60 min Jeff leads
TUE

Day 2 — Tuesday

Seller welcome calls + baseline assessment

Each seller gets a 30-minute conversation — not a review, a conversation. What do they love about selling? Where do they feel stuck? What do they wish leadership understood? A ProPresence baseline assessment follows: communication style, stress response, and emotional self-awareness.

Sellers · 30 min each ProPresence baseline Jeff leads
WED

Day 3 — Wednesday

Shadow day — observe before you coach

Jeff reviews 2–3 call transcripts or email threads per seller. No feedback yet — just pattern recognition. Talk/listen ratios, discovery depth, objection handling, emotional tone. Jeff annotates for the context tools can't read.

Call + email analysis Jeff reviews
THU

Day 4 — Thursday

Daily rhythm goes live

Sellers receive their first structured daily check-in. One priority, one focus question, one accountability prompt. They respond in a few sentences. The pattern builds from day one.

FRI

Day 5 — Friday

Diagnostic readout with the owner

A plain-language summary: each seller's strengths, their specific coaching priorities, the messaging gaps we found, and the 30-day plan. No deck, no jargon. Just clarity. You leave knowing exactly what we're going to fix and how.
